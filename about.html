About Me

I’m a Software Engineer who brings a disciplined, professional mindset influenced by preparing for a highly structured law enforcement career and by my respect for the standards upheld by the Connecticut State Police. That process reinforced the importance of accountability, attention to detail, and professionalism — qualities I carry into my work as an engineer. I specialize in full-stack development with a strong focus on backend systems, authentication, and secure application design, while also contributing to responsive and accessible frontends. I build scalable web applications using JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB, and I’m seeking roles as a Full-Stack Developer or Software Engineer where I can deliver secure, maintainable, production-ready solutions.

With the people I call my sisters — a reminder that trust, teamwork, and support are essential both in life and in engineering teams.
